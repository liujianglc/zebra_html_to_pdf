<p><b>Designed for voice in your loudest environments</b></p>
<p>The very loud front facing speaker and instantly accessible volume</p>
<p>control make it easy to hear scanner feedback and</p>

<p><b>Fits in virtually any warehouse/material handling vehicle</b></p>
<p>Multiple mounting options, including standard RAM mounts</p>
<p>combine with a compact size for an easy fit and easy mounting,</p>
<p>even in the smallest vehicles.</p>
<p><b>Power to support today&#x2019;s graphics-intensive applications</b></p>
<p>Get the power you need to deliver blazing speed for your</p>
<p>applications with your choice of a dual core 1.33 GHz with</p>
<p>2GB RAM or a quad core 1.91 GHz processor with 4GB RAM.</p>
<p>Superior flexibility &#x2014; add bar code scanning, voice and more</p>
<p>Different workers need different capabilities. That&#x2019;s why the</p>
<p>VC80 offers all the ports you need to add bar code scanners,</p>
<p>mobile printers, a push-to-talk microphone and more.</p>
<p><b>Rock-solid fast wireless connectivity</b></p>
<p>With support for the latest wireless standards &#x2014; 802.11a/b/g/n/ac</p>
<p>and Bluetooth 4.0 &#x2014; plus a choice of internal or external antennas,</p>
<p>the VC80 can give your workers/deliver desktop wired-line style</p>
<p>performance on the most complex applications.</p>
<p><b>Repairable, right on the spot</b></p>
<p>Since the touchscreen &#x2014; the most frequently damaged component</p>
<p>&#x2014; is field replaceable, trips to the service depot</p>
<p>are eliminated, increasing device availability and your return</p>
<p>on investment.</p>
<p><b>Pair Bluetooth devices in a split second</b></p>
<p>Need a separate handheld scanner? With a quick scan of the</p>
<p>pairing bar code printed on the VC80 with Zebra&#x2019;s cordless LS3478</p>
<p>or DS3578 scanners, your workers are up and running.</p>
<p>Comprehensive Windows operating system (OS) support</p>
<p>The VC80 supports Embedded Standard 7 or Windows 7</p>
<p>Professional, and is Windows 10 ready, making it easy to extend</p>
<p>virtually any PC-based app to your warehouse vehicles.</p>
